About

I was born and raised at Lake Tahoe, California and growing up I have always had a passion for video games, sports, and technology. At the age of 16 I decided my dream was to become a game developer. This lead me to enroll in the Computer Science and Engineering program at the University of Nevada-Reno. I am now a few months away from receiveing my CSE degree with a minor in Math and now looking for my dream job of becoming a game developer.

After attending many CS classes I have realized my passion in CS is in Graphics, Engine Architecture, and Design. In graphics, I have experince in OpenGL and GLSL including lighting and physics I am also currently getting practice with DirectX and HLSL programming. In Game Engine Architecture, I have created a small engine for my graphics projects as well as created an engine utilizing the OGRE graphics engine. I also love to play around with new engines currently I am using Amazons Lumberyard to create some levels, but I have also used Unity and Unreal to create some projects in 2D, 3D, and VR. In Design, I have created a different types of games and levels in Unity. Most of the work I have designed levels in have been 2D with my original art, but am currently Working on 3D level in Unity with VR and a 3D level with my engine built with OGRE.
